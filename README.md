# Instagram Content Scheduler

Welcome to the **instagram-content-scheduler** repository! This project is dedicated to helping users effectively manage their Instagram content scheduling through automation. With the increasing demand for social media presence, especially on platforms like Instagram, having a reliable tool for content scheduling is essential for marketers and content creators alike.

## Features

### Instagram Auto-Post

The **Instagram Auto-Post** feature of MadHub allows users to automate their posting tasks directly on their Instagram profiles. This is particularly beneficial for content creators looking to maintain a consistent posting schedule without the manual effort of logging in at specified times. 

#### Usage Scenarios
- **Ideal for marketers and influencers** who need to keep their audience engaged by publishing content at optimal times.
- **Suitable for businesses** looking to regularly promote products or services through engaging posts while optimizing their marketing efforts.

#### Operation Steps
1. **Select the Input Mode or File Mode**: You can either type your captions directly or upload a file containing your content.
2. **Set Post Visibility**: Choose whether to publish your posts publicly or limit visibility to followers.
3. **Configure Posting Schedule**: Specify the operation interval to control how frequently posts are made (e.g., daily, weekly).
4. **Insert Images**: Utilize the ability to insert images along with your captions to enhance engagement.

By using MadHub's **Instagram Auto-Post** feature, you can enhance your account activity, maintain a steady flow of content, and free up time for other activities.

### Instagram Account Warm-Up

MadHub’s **Instagram Account Warm-Up** feature is designed to nurture new accounts or rehabilitate older ones by simulating authentic user interactions. This is crucial in establishing trust and credibility on the platform.

#### Usage Scenarios
- **Perfect for new accounts** that require gradual interaction to avoid potential bans.
- **Effective for older accounts** that need to regain visibility and trust within the community.

#### Operation Steps
1. **Simulate User Behavior**: The tool mimics real user activity by browsing home feeds and video pages.
2. **Set Interaction Probability**: Adjust settings to control how often to like or comment on posts, allowing for a more natural behavioral pattern.
3. **Distribution Settings**: Configure execution probability distributions to manage how interactions are spread out over time.

With the **Instagram Account Warm-Up** feature, MadHub helps improve account visibility, reduces the risk of account suspension, and builds a stronger social media presence.

## Installation Instructions

MadHub is a user-friendly Android social media automation tool that can be installed on real Android devices, cloud phones, or emulators. The graphical interface requires no coding knowledge, making it accessible for all users. Furthermore, MadHub supports 24/7 automated operations, allowing you to manage your social media presence effortlessly.
